Personnel

Demaree Services is owned and operated by John and Bonnie Demaree.

Employees at our company are issued Nextel phones to assure 24/7 accessibility. We recognize that reaching the right person in the middle of the night can be difficult, so our company phone number always pages the on-duty supervisor for immediate response. Our staff comes from an emergency response background and strives to be always ready.

Demaree Services has staff available to assist in electrical, custodial, landscaping, maintenance, and repair work.
